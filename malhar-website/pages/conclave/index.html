CONCLAVE

Conclave Schedule

Keynote Speaker

Lt Gen Satish Dua

Lieutenant General Satish Dua, PVSM, UYSM, SM, VSM is a retired General officer of the Indian Army who has also served as the Chief of Integrated Defence Staff. As a counter-terrorism specialist, he was the Corps Commander in Kashmir, who planned and executed the Surgical Strikes in response to the terror attack at Uri. As a member of the National Crisis Management Group, he has also coordinated the efforts of the Army, Navy & Air Force in National Crises & Disaster situations.

Keynote Interview

Dr John Lennox

Professor John Lennox is a mathematician, philosopher of science, and Christian apologist who is an Emeritus professor of mathematics at the University of Oxford. He is the author of the books, Can Science Explain Everything?, Where is God in a Coronavirus World? He has also published over seventy mathematical papers and co-authored two Oxford Mathematical Monographs.

General Speaker Session

Mr Sudhir Sitapati

Mr Sudhir Sitapati is the author of the best-selling book 'The CEO Factory: Management lessons from Hindustan Unilever'. He was the executive director of Hindustan Unilever Ltd. Management Committee and is credited to have built the company’s foods and refreshment business as one of the largest in India. Godrej Consumer Products has appointed him as its managing director and chief executive officer (CEO) with effect from October 18, 2021.

Panel Discussion

Mr Anurag Chauhan

Mr Anurag Chauhan is the founder of Humans For Humanity, widely known for social work, particularly with regards to menstrual hygiene management. In 2015, he started a project called WASH that has reached over 2 million women. He has been awarded the Karamveer Chakra by the United Nations. He also runs awareness programs in many villages in India.

Ms Sumita Ghose

Ms Sumita Ghose is the founder and managing director of Rangsutra, which works towards socio-economic development and inclusive growth in rural India. Rangasutra is committed to ensuring sustainable livelihoods for rural artisans in India. Ms Ghose was chosen to receive the Nari Shakti Puraskar on International Women's Day in 2016. She has also received the MacArthur Fellowship for Leadership and is a member of the CII - Aspen India Fellows' first cohort.

Dr Urvashi Sahni

Dr Urvashi Sahni is one of the most influential social entrepreneurs in India in the field of teacher training and girl’s education. She has impacted the lives of millions of teachers and students and also serves as the Chief Executive and Founder of SHEF (Study Hall Educational Foundation). She is also the founder of SURAKSHA and Sisters in Solidarity

Interactive Session

Ms Anita Nair

Anita Nair is an Indian novelist, a bestselling and critically acclaimed author of the novels, The Better Man, Ladies Coupé, and Mistress. She has also written poetry, essays, crime series, short stories, historical fiction, romance, and children's literature. Anita Nair has been awarded The Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award ( 2012) and The Hindu Literary Prize (2014) for her tremendous contributions to the literary world.

Performance Session

Abhijeet Srivastava

Abhijeet Srivastava is a renowned singer, composer and songwriter. He is best known for his playback singing in the movie Andhadhun and for the song Chashni from Bharat. His accomplishments include being the finalist of ‘Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Lil champs’ and bagging the Mirchi music award for the best male upcoming artist.

Imcha Imchen

Imcha Imchen is a multilingual singer songwriter and music producer from Nagaland. He released his debut music video “Moving On” at the tender age of 18 and his single "Spare My Heart" was No. 16 in Apple's Top 100 Songs for 2020. His emotive vocals add to the pictoral experience that strike the listener as a beautiful amalgamation.
